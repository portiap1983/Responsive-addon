Why Vancouver

Something fun about Vancouver

Vancouver, a bustling west coast seaport in British Columbia, is among Canada’s densest, most ethnically diverse cities. A popular filming location, it’s surrounded by mountains, and also has thriving art, theatre and music scenes.

Virtuous Pie

Virtuous Pie was born in Vancouver, founded by passionate vegan foodies Rob Milne and his sons, Ben and Alex. Their mission was simple yet powerful: to create food that not only tastes incredible but is also good for our bodies and the planet.

M!LA

MILA was created to prove that doing good for the planet doesn't have to come with compromise. Through trusted culinary innovation and a focus on internationally-inspired flavour, MILA delivers an elevated, yet approachable, plant-based dining experience.

Viet Family

This family-run eatery serves up classic Vietnamese rolls, rice dishes, and noodle soups in a cozy, no-frills space. Located on the bustling Commercial Drive in Vancouver, it’s a go-to spot for fresh, flavorful, and comforting Vietnamese cuisine.

Address:

1414 Commercial Dr, Vancouver, BC V5L 3X9
